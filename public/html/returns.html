Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not, we're here to help.

Extended Holiday Returns: Items purchased between November 1st and December 31st can be returned until January 31st of the following year.

Our Return Policy

30-Day Return Window

You have 30 days from the delivery date to return your item(s) for a full refund or exchange.

Free Returns

We offer free returns on all domestic orders. International returns are subject to shipping fees.

Full Refund

Receive a full refund to your original payment method. Processing may take 5-7 business days.

How to Return an Item

1

Start Your Return

Log in to your account and go to Order History. Select the item(s) you'd like to return and follow the prompts.

Start Return
2

Pack & Ship

Pack your return using the original packaging if possible. Include the return label and place it securely on the package.

3

Track & Receive

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location. You'll receive an email when we process your return.

Return Conditions

Items must be:

  • In original condition with all tags attached
  • Unworn, unwashed, and free of stains or odors
  • In the original packaging with all accessories included
  • Accompanied by the original receipt or order number

Non-returnable items:

  • Final sale items (marked as such on the product page)
  • Gift cards and e-gift cards
  • Personalized or customized items
  • Items damaged due to wear and tear
  • Items without original packaging or tags

Exchanges

We currently don't offer direct exchanges. To exchange an item, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the desired item. This ensures you receive your new item as quickly as possible.

International Returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs and any applicable customs fees. We recommend using a trackable shipping method and purchasing shipping insurance, as we cannot be responsible for lost or damaged return packages.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our Customer Service team within 7 days of delivery. We'll provide a prepaid return label and process a replacement or refund as soon as possible.
